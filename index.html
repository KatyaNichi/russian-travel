What didn't we see there?

According to surveys, 95% of Russians dream of going somewhere, but only 36% plan to spend their holidays in their home country. Like, what haven’t we seen here at home? In fact, Russia is a whole universe with the gentle sea of the south, the dense forests of the Sayan Mountains and the harsh ice of the Putorana plateau. And you can see all these beauties without millions in your account, a foreign passport and many hours of flights. Like, for example, Vera Bashmakova, a brave young mother who grabbed three children, put them in her Lada and drove 20 thousand kilometers around her native country. We have selected and described some interesting places worthy of your holiday.

Altai

url

Facebook

Altai is one of the most beautiful places in Russia. Primarily because of the mountains: if you drive along the ridge, you will see slopes strewn with pine trees, mountain rivers and lakes. And if you open the windows in your car, you can get acquainted with the invisible The miracle of these places is the mountain air.

The climate in Altai is moderate, so it is best to go here in the summer. So you will see everything diversity of local flora and fauna. Moose roam the forests of Altai and fly over the ridges eagles, and roe deer graze on the plains. And the famous Pallas' cats are also inhabitants of the Altai the edges.

Winter Baikal

url

https://vk.com/baikalmile

Everyone knows Baikal as the largest lake in the world. Many also know that this is the largest source of fresh water and one of the the most beautiful places in Russia.

Of course, this is all true. But Baikal is also an ideal place for skioring competitions. This is a sport where the skier ties himself to a motorcycle and the tandem tries develop as much speed as possible on the ice. In March 2019 at the festival The Baikal Mile set a world record of 197.011 km/h.

Siberia ends not in the Urals, but in Karelia: the Siberian larch that forms the taiga does not grow west of Vodlozero. But here it reaches 30 meters - Karelian forests National parks, due to impassable swamps, have never known the axe. Some pine trees for more than half a millennium. Touch a living being who has seen the sun before, Ivan the Terrible saw him. In a virgin forest you won’t find a trail for a hundred kilometers. And on rare paths there are trees a couple of meters from the ground are marked with bear claws. So that everyone knows who is the boss here.
